# 📊 Customer Insights & Business Recommendation Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Customer Insights & Business Recommendation Dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Power BI**. The dashboard analyzes customer behavior, sales performance, product performance, customer segmentation, and profitability to support data-driven business decisions.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
* Monitor sales and profit performance.
* Identify top-performing products and categories.
* Compare sales across different regions.
* Segment customers based on their value.
* Generate actionable business recommendations.

## 📂 Dataset

* **Dataset Name:** Customer Insights Dataset
* **Rows:** 96,000
* **Format:** CSV
* **Source:** Synthetic dataset created for business analytics and dashboard development.

### Dataset Features

* Customer ID
* Order ID
* Order Date
* Region
* Category
* Product
* Quantity
* Unit Price
* Sales
* Profit
* Customer Segment
* Customer Type
* Discount Percentage
* Gender

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards (Total Sales, Profit, Customers, Orders, Average Order Value)
* Customer Overview
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Region-wise Sales Analysis
* Top Selling Products
* Category-wise Sales
* Profit by Product
* Customer Segmentation
* Discount Effectiveness Analysis
* Gender Analysis
* Interactive Slicers

## 💡 Business Insights

* Returning customers contribute significantly to revenue.
* Top-selling products generate the highest sales and profit.
* High-performing regions drive overall business growth.
* High-value customers contribute a major share of revenue.
* Moderate discounts improve sales while maintaining profitability.

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

* Improve customer retention through loyalty programs.
* Invest more in high-performing regions.
* Focus marketing efforts on top-selling products.
* Optimize pricing and discount strategies.
* Monitor KPIs regularly for better decision-making.
